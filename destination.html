Hakone

Hakone is famous for it onsen. Also right next to Mt. Fuji. If you want to go to Hakone you need to take a bullet train. Bullet train allows you to see other scenaries before arriving to Hakone.

Studio Ghibli Museum

This image is by:Japanese Guide found on japanesesearch.com/ghibli-museum/

All character from Miyazaki's movies are present here. If you love Miyazaki's work then come to Studio Ghibli Museum. To get to Studio Ghibli Museum you must order you ticket in advance if you want to really go there. The ticket will sell out fast because the Museum is very popular.
